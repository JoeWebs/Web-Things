Tips for media on a site

My best tip for media be it video, pictures ect. Is to put them on a site that holds media. For videos use youtube as a video storage area. Then I can embed the video into the site without using disk space. For pictures I recommend photobucket. I can get the url for the photo and link it directly into the site.
Just for the videos upload them to youtube and set the video as private then give me the embed link and we are set. For the pictures upload them to photobucket and give methe name and I will pull the url from it and put it onto the site
